About VIDHAI

VIDHAI (Virtual Intelligent Digital Helper for Agricultural Innovation) is a digital platform created to transform farming in Kerala. It connects traditional farming practices with modern technology to solve the key challenges farmers face today.

Core Objectives

  • Smart Technology for Farmers: Easy-to-use digital tools to support Kerala‚Äôs farming community.
  • Data-Driven Support: Providing farmers and experts with clear, useful insights for better decisions.
  • Sustainable Farming: Promoting eco-friendly and cost-effective farming practices.
  • Voice & Chat Support: Farmers can use voice-to-text (microphone) for easy communication, and get instant help through our AI-powered chatbot.

Our Mission

To build a simple, farmer-friendly platform that brings together farmers, technology, and innovation ‚Äî making agriculture in Kerala smarter, sustainable, and future-ready.
